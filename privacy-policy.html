Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

Vitarc ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered nutrition, fitness, and health tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
  • Email address and/or Google profile (name, email, avatar) when you sign in
Health & Body Data
  • Body weight, body measurements, and body-fat percentage
  • Steps and active calories burned (synced from Apple Health or Google Health Connect)
  • Intermittent fasting windows
  • Water intake logs
Nutrition Data
  • Meal logs (food items, calories, macronutrients)
  • Meal plans and grocery lists
  • Barcode scan results from the Open Food Facts database
Photos & Media
  • Progress photos (front, side, back) stored locally on your device
  • Meal photos submitted for AI-powered food recognition
Device & Usage Information
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Crash logs and general usage analytics (collected by Expo)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your daily calorie and macronutrient targets
  • Generate personalized meal plans and grocery lists
  • Provide AI-powered food recognition from photos and text
  • Sync health data between the app and Apple Health / Google Health Connect
  • Display your weight and body-composition trends over time
  • Authenticate your account and sync data across devices

3. Third-Party Services

Vitarc relies on the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy:

Google Gemini AI

Meal photos and text descriptions are sent to the Gemini API for food recognition and nutritional analysis. Gemini API Terms

Supabase

Authentication, user profiles, and cloud-synced data are stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database. Supabase Privacy Policy

Apple HealthKit (iOS)

With your permission, the app reads steps, active calories, and weight from Apple Health, and may write weight and nutrition data back. Apple HealthKit data is never sent to our servers or any third party. Apple Privacy Policy

Google Health Connect (Android)

With your permission, the app reads steps, active calories, and weight from Health Connect. Health Connect data is processed locally on your device. Google Privacy Policy

Open Food Facts

Barcode scans query the Open Food Facts public API to retrieve product nutritional data. No personal data is sent. Open Food Facts Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Local data: Meal logs, progress photos, and preferences are stored in an on-device SQLite database and local file storage. This data remains on your device unless you opt in to cloud sync.
  • Cloud data: If you sign in, your profile and synced data are stored in Supabase for as long as your account is active.
  • AI processing: Meal photos sent for AI recognition are processed in real time and are not stored by us after the response is returned.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data associated with your account.
  • Delete — request complete deletion of your account and all cloud-stored data.
  • Revoke permissions — withdraw HealthKit or Health Connect access at any time through your device's system settings.
  • Delete local data — uninstalling the app removes all locally-stored data, including photos, meal logs, and settings.

To request data access or deletion, contact us at srikark283@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Vitarc is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at srikark283@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Vitarc ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Vitarc, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, as well as data generated through your use of the App.

Personal & Account Data
  • Identity Data: Name and email address provided via Google Sign-In or manual registration.
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, height, and activity levels used to calculate metabolic rates.
Health & Fitness Data (Sensitive Information)
  • Body Metrics: Weight, body fat percentage, and physical measurements.
  • Activity Data: Steps and active energy burned, retrieved from Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect.
  • Logging: Water intake, fasting durations, and exercise logs.
Nutrition & AI Data
  • Dietary Logs: Foods consumed, caloric intake, and macronutrient breakdowns.
  • Visual Data: Images of meals uploaded for AI-powered recognition.
  • Barcode Data: Product identifiers used to query nutritional databases.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data based on Contractual Necessity (to provide the service) and Legitimate Interests (to improve the app). Uses include:

  • Calculating personalized nutritional targets (TDEE, Macros).
  • Providing AI-driven meal analysis and automated grocery list generation.
  • Synchronizing data across multiple devices via your secure account.
  • Generating progress charts and health trend visualizations.
  • Internal debugging, crash reporting, and performance optimization.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing & Services

We only share your data with third-party service providers necessary for App functionality:

AI Processing (Google Gemini)

Meal photos and text queries are processed via the Google Gemini API. Data sent to the API is used for real-time analysis and is governed by Google’s Enterprise Privacy terms, which generally ensure data is not used to train global models without consent.

Infrastructure (Supabase)

Your profile and encrypted health logs are stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. View Privacy Policy.

Health Ecosystems (Apple & Google)

Access to HealthKit and Health Connect is optional. We strictly adhere to the Apple HealthKit Framework and Google Health Connect policies. Your health data is never used for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account within the App settings at any time.
  • Local Storage: Progress photos and cached meal logs are stored locally. These are erased when the App is uninstalled.
  • Deletion Requests: Upon account deletion, all personal data stored in our cloud databases (Supabase) is permanently purged within 30 days.

5. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a digital export of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate health or profile data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the "right to be forgotten" and have your data deleted.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke camera or health permissions via system settings at any time.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and secure their devices.

7. Children's Privacy

Vitarc is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such data collection, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
